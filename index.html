📊 Columns Explained
Price The stock's latest closing price in USD (or local currency for EU stocks).
1D Change How much the price moved today vs yesterday's close. ▲ +5% means it went up 5%. ▼ -5% means it dropped 5%.
5D Move Price change over the last 5 trading days (roughly 1 week). Tells you if today's move is part of a bigger trend or just a one-day event.
Volume How many more people than usual are trading this stock today. 1.0× = normal. 3.0× = 3 times the usual activity. Higher = more attention.
🚦 Signals
PRICE MOVE The stock moved more than the threshold in a single day (≥1.5% relaxed / ≥3% strict). Something caused a notable price swing.
VOL SPIKE Trading activity is unusually high compared to the 10-day average. Often means news hit, or big investors are moving in/out.
MOMENTUM The stock has been trending in the same direction for 5 days. The move isn't just today — there's a pattern building.
💡 Tip: Stocks with 2–3 signals are the strongest candidates. All three signals agreeing is rare and worth attention.
📶 Volume Bar Colors
Blue Normal to moderate spike — 1× to 2.5× the usual volume. Nothing unusual.
Orange High spike — 2.5× to 4× normal volume. Elevated interest, worth noting.
Red Extreme spike — 4×+ normal volume. Major trading activity. Something significant is happening.
⚠️ Volume color is about intensity only — not direction. A red bar on a rising stock means it's surging on huge volume. Same bar on a falling stock means it's crashing on huge volume.
⚙️ Filter Modes
Relaxed Shows anything with at least one signal (≥1.5% move OR ≥1.3× volume). Casts a wide net — expect more results, more noise.
Strict Only shows stocks with both a big price move (≥3%) AND a volume spike (≥2×) on the same day. Fewer results, higher conviction.
💡 Start with Relaxed to see the full picture. Switch to Strict when you want only the strongest, clearest signals.
↕️ Direction Filter
Both Shows all movers regardless of direction. Best for a general market overview.
Movers Up ↑ Only stocks that went up today. Use this to find stocks gaining strength or breaking out.
Movers Down ↓ Only stocks that went down today. Use this to spot weakness or stocks under selling pressure.
🔄 Auto-Scan
What it does Automatically re-runs the scan every 10 minutes so you don't have to manually refresh. A countdown shows when the next scan fires.
NEW badge After an auto-scan, any stock that wasn't in the previous results gets a NEW badge so you can spot fresh signals immediately.
Yellow flash Rows that were already in the list but had their price or signals change will briefly flash yellow to draw your eye to what updated.
